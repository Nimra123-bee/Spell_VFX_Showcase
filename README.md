# Spell Effects VFX Showcase

This repository showcases a stylized spell system developed during my internship at Codematics, designed around a complete gameplay feedback loop: energy buildup, release, and impact.

The effects are built to simulate area-based magical attacks with strong visual storytelling and satisfying impact.

## 🎯 Design Focus

The entire system was built on a core principle:

**Energy Consumption → Energy Release → Impact**

Each spell visually communicates this sequence, making the action feel intentional and powerful rather than random.

Effects include:
- energy gathering at a central point  
- directional and radial spell release  
- large-area impact designed for enemy clusters  

---

## 🧠 My Contribution

- Designed a complete spell system including casting and hit effects  
- Created arrow-based spell mechanics with multiple behaviors:
  - vertical drop attacks  
  - circular area attacks  
  - sinusoidal motion patterns  
- Focused on visual storytelling through timing and motion  
- Developed impact effects to reinforce gameplay feedback  
- Tuned animation timing and color transitions for satisfying results  
- Built all effects using Particle System and Shader Graph (excluding textures)  

---

## ⚙️ Tools & Techniques

- Unity Particle System (timing, emission control, motion behavior)  
- Shader Graph (energy flow and visual enhancement)  
- Post Processing (global visual polish)  

---

## 🧩 Key Challenge

The main challenge was making spells feel satisfying and meaningful rather than just visual noise.

This required:
- carefully designing the sequence of buildup, release, and impact  
- balancing timing between phases  
- ensuring each variation felt unique in behavior, not just appearance
